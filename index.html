Some worlds do not return.

They do not retrace their own steps.

They wait in the dark.

They gather shape again.

Old echoes fade into new stone.

Broken rules become cleaner paths.

Familiar tension finds a sharper edge.

A new world is opening.

It is built for the moment.

Before the door gives way.

For the weight of what you carry.

For the choice to press deeper.

Or make it out alive.

It is a world of risk,

consequence,

hidden histories,

and light.

Found only after you have earned it.

If you were with us before,

you may recognize the feeling.

Before you recognize the place.

If this is your first time.

At the threshold,

welcome.

More will be revealed soon.

We are back!
